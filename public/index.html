The History of Elephant Leg Brand

Around the mid 16th century, the original Muay Thai boxers began bathing in a river near a salt mine. The fighters noticed a reduction in muscle soreness and a faster recovery time between days of training and fights.

They soon attributed these benefits to runoff water from the salt mine into the river. Soon after, the fighters collected these salts and began using them as part of their recovery regimens.
